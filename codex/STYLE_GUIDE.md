# Style Guide

## Language

Default language for international materials: English.

Secondary language: Croatian.

Use Croatian for local outreach when appropriate, especially for Croatian universities and professional schools.

## Writing Principles

- Start with value, not biography.
- Position Aron as an industry expert, not as a job seeker.
- Emphasize practical teaching, laboratories and enterprise scenarios.
- Use clear, credible and specific language.
- Prefer concise paragraphs and strong section headings.

## Preferred Phrases

- External lecturer
- Industry expert
- Practical enterprise education
- Real-world infrastructure scenarios
- AI-assisted IT operations
- Enterprise technology education
- Hands-on laboratories
- Digital transformation in practice

## Avoid

- I am looking for a job
- I would like to apply for employment
- Professor as a formal title
- Overly generic AI language
- Long unsupported claims

## Content Structure

Most documents should follow this structure:

1. Who Aron is
2. What value he brings
3. What he can teach
4. Why it matters now
5. Suggested collaboration format
6. Contact

## Collaboration Formats

Use these consistently:

- guest lecture
- short workshop
- elective course
- lifelong learning program
- professional education program
- industry case study lecture

## Course Writing Format

Each course should include:

- course title
- short description
- target audience
- learning outcomes
- duration
- labs
- tools and technologies
