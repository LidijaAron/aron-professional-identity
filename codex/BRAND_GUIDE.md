# Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

Professional, practical and industry-oriented.

Aron Margetić should be presented as an experienced enterprise technology educator who connects academic learning with real-world IT operations.

## Core Positioning

**Industry Expert in Enterprise AI, Infrastructure and Digital Transformation**

Alternative titles:

- Enterprise AI Educator
- Senior IT Specialist
- External Lecturer
- Enterprise Technology Educator

## Key Message

> Bridging Enterprise IT, Artificial Intelligence and Higher Education.

## Tone

Use a tone that is:

- professional
- confident
- concise
- practical
- credible
- education-oriented

Avoid:

- sounding like a job applicant
- exaggerated claims
- unsupported academic titles
- buzzword-heavy marketing language

## Visual Style

Preferred style:

- modern consulting style
- Microsoft Learn / AWS Academy inspired
- clean white space
- dark blue, white and light gray
- professional headshot
- cards, metrics and clear sections

## Color Palette

- Navy: `#0B1F3A`
- Blue: `#2563EB`
- Light blue: `#DBEAFE`
- Gray: `#F3F4F6`
- Text: `#111827`
- Muted text: `#6B7280`

## Typography

Use system fonts where possible:

- Segoe UI
- Calibri
- Arial
- Helvetica

## Signature Metrics

Use these consistently:

- 25+ years professional education
- 2000+ trained students and professionals
- 20+ years enterprise IT
- Senior IT Specialist
- Lecturer at Algebra Bernays University

## Do Not Use

Do not use `Professor` as a formal title unless the context clearly means lecturer/instructor and not academic rank.
